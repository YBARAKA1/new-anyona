How we are

Formed in 1996, Aristocrats Concrete Limited operates a quarry at Katani, Syokimau, equipped with the latest infrastructure that conforms to industry standards in the manufacture and production of construction materials such as ballast, quarry sand, rock dust, and more. Our main crushing plant has a capacity of over 200 tonnes per hour, meeting the needs of our ever-growing customer base. We are also equipped with a hydrocone with a capacity of 100 tonnes per hour for double-crushed materials for road contractors. Additionally, our sand washing machine has a capacity of 100 tonnes per hour.
